Muthiingini High School is a Kenyan Secondary school that is designated as a Public Institution.
It is located in Kibwezi constituency, Muthingiini Sub-location, Nzambani Location in Mtito andei
Division of Makueni District. This is within the Eastern Province of Kenya. The student population
comprises of Mixed Students and the current status puts it as an/a Ordinary School. The learning
option for students includes Day and Boarding. Studies are carried out through a Kenyan based Curriculum.
